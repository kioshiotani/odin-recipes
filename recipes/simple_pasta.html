Description

Fast and Simple Garlic Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 5 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • salt
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Smash the garic with the salt
  2. In a frying pan put the crushed garlic and the oil
  3. Fry over medium heat, being careful not to burn the garlic, just brown it
  4. Add the butter and let it simmer briefly, stirring constantly
  5. Add the result to the pasta
