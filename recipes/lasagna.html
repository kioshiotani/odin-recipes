Lasagna Recipe

Image by timolina on Freepik

Description

This lasagna recipe is very easy to follow and the result is satisfactory.

Ingredients

  • 500g of lasagna dough
  • 2 boxes of milk cream
  • 3 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • 500g of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 glasses of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 3 gloves of crushed garlic
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 500g of ham
  • salt
  • 1 grate onion
  • 1 box of tomato drip
  • 1 packet of grated cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the dough following the manufacturer instructions
  2. Sauté the garlic, onion, ground beef and tomato sauce; cook for 3 minutes and set aside
  3. Melt the butter, add the flour and mix
  4. Pour the milk in gradually and continue stirring
  5. Add the cream, stir for 1 minute, and turn off the heat
  6. Mount the lasagna as you want
